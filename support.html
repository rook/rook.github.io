Support

The Rook community is here to support you:

  • Ask questions in our community forums in Slack or GitHub Discussions
  • To request a feature or report a defect, open a GitHub Issue
  • To contribute to the project, see the Contribution Guide
  • For commercial support check out the list of Rook-related support and services companies below.

Commercial Support and Services

Clyso

As a premium partner of Ceph, Clyso provides expert support, integration, development, and consulting services specifically for Rook and Ceph in large enterprise environments. Clyso’s managed services encompass hybrid cloud solutions utilizing open-source technologies, including Kubernetes, Rook Ceph, and more, ensuring flexibility and eliminating vendor lock-ins.
Additionally, Clyso offers advanced backup solutions, comprehensive cloud migration support, and digitalization services through microservices.
Services include:

  • Managed Ceph and Rook service, consulting and support
  • Ceph Full System Analysis: helps fine tune your Rook and Ceph clusters for optimal performance
  • Clyso Enterprise Storage powered by Ceph
  • Emergency Support

Red Hat

Red Hat® OpenShift® Data Foundation is software-defined storage for containers. Red Hat OpenShift Data Foundation helps teams develop and deploy applications with storage quickly and efficiently across clouds. Red Hat OpenShift Data Foundation is included with Red Hat OpenShift Platform Plus, a complete set of powerful, optimized tools to secure, protect, and manage your apps.

  • Based on Rook, Ceph, and other open source projects
  • Fully supported Enterprise solution
